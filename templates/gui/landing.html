This is a simple demo of the A.L.F.R.E.D. system
You are presented with information about a series of space objects.
For each object, decide whether to investigate it or not.
You can make up your own rule (e.g. only investigate unkown objects).
A.L.F.R.E.D. will try to learn your rule and recommend actions by highlighting them in green; the greener the action, the more certain A.L.F.R.E.D. is.
